What is Coronary Calcium?

Calcium Deposits that build up in ones arteries as a result of heart disease. Higher levels of Coronary Calcium indicate that a person may be at a higher risk for a Stroke or Heart Attack.


Risk Associated with Different Coronary Calcium Scores

Risk levels are used to detect the likelyhood of the prescence of Coronary Artery Disease in the patient.

Score Evidence of CAD
0 None
1 - 10 Minimal
11 - 100 Mild
101 - 400 Moderate
Over 400 Extensive
