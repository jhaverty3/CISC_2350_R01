Medicine

Data Mining is used extensively in medicine due to the large sets of data collected in both research projects
and primary care appointments. Within the field of medicine, applications of Data Mining include......

  • Computational Phenotyping
  • Diagnosis of Disease
  • Tumor Classification
  • Early Warning Detection Systems
  • Drug Discovery

The hope is that as computational power and available Datasets expand,
the efficacy of these technologies will expand as well.
The implied consequence being better medical treatment methods and disease detection.
