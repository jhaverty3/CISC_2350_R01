Neural Networks

Neural Networks are a method of unsupervised learning that was inspired by the way actual biological
nervous systems learn from their environments. Recently, Neural Networks with multiple hidden layers have made huge
advancements in the field of Artificial Intelligence. They are being used for Computer Vision, Robotics and Natural
Language Processing. Advancements in these field in the future hope to yield tremendous results for Data Mining
in health care as data will be able to be collected by computers both visually and auditorily.
